About SUCOFINDO

Learn more about our company history, vision and mission

Our History

PT SUCOFINDO (Persero) was established on October 22, 1956 as the first inspection company in Indonesia. Initially, SUCOFINDO was established to support the inspection needs of the export-import industry in Indonesia.

Over time, SUCOFINDO has developed into a company that provides comprehensive services in the fields of inspection, testing, certification and verification with international standards.

Vision & Mission

Vision

To become a leading and trusted inspection, testing, certification and verification company in Indonesia and internationally.

Mission

  • Provide excellent service with international standards
  • Support national industry development
  • Commitment to quality, integrity and professionalism
  • Continuous innovation in service development
