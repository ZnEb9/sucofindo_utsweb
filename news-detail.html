SUCOFINDO Wins Best Inspection Company Award 2023

By: Admin SUCOFINDO Category: Achievement

PT SUCOFINDO (Persero) has been awarded as the Best Inspection Company in Indonesia for the year 2023 by the Ministry of Industry. The award was presented directly by the Minister of Industry at a ceremony held in Jakarta on October 14, 2023.

This award is given in appreciation of SUCOFINDO's consistent performance in providing high-quality inspection services that meet international standards, as well as its contribution to supporting Indonesia's industrial sector.

Recognition of Excellence

The selection process for this award was rigorous, evaluating various aspects including service quality, customer satisfaction, innovation, and contribution to national industry development. SUCOFINDO scored highly in all assessment categories.

"This award is a testament to the hard work and dedication of all SUCOFINDO employees who have consistently maintained high service standards," said John Doe, President Director of SUCOFINDO, when receiving the award.

"We are honored to receive this recognition. It will motivate us to continue improving our services and supporting the growth of Indonesia's industry through reliable inspection, testing, and certification services."

- John Doe, President Director

Future Plans

With this achievement, SUCOFINDO plans to:

  • Expand its service network to more regions in Indonesia
  • Invest in new testing equipment and technology
  • Enhance employee competency through training programs
  • Develop new services to meet evolving industry needs

The company also announced plans to open three new branch offices in 2024 to better serve customers across the archipelago.
